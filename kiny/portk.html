IBIRAMBUYE KURI ISANGE BAR-RESTO

Uru nu urubuga rw'ibanze rwa Isange Bar-Resto, resto-bar iherereye mu mugi wa kigali itanga amafunguro mu mitekere yo mu mico y'ibihugu bitandukanye. Yashinzwe na Shefu Jean Pierre Mutabazi, wabonye impamyabumenyi ya PHD mu by'ubutetsi yakuye mu gihugu cy'ubwongereza muri kaminuza ikomeye cyane ya OXFORD. Isange Bar-Resto ikoresha abakozi barenga 120 barimo abashefu bakuru,abashefu basanzwe,abaseriveri ndetse n'abandi. aho kandi haba harimo aba shefu b'abanyamwuga bakomoka mu bihugu bitandukanye,ibyo bigafasha mu gutanga amafunguro ari mu mico y'imitekere inyuranye. byinshi mu binyobwa ndetse n'amadeseri bikorwa n'abatetsi b'abanyarwanda b'inzobere ndetse bikagira uburyohe buhebuje. Duhora twifuza kumva ibitekerezo cyangwa inyunganizi byanyu nkabakiriya bacu kugira ngo dukomeze kubaha seivisi inoze.
